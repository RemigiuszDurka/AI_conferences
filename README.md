# Using AI for Didactics and Research

A comprehensive course on applying Artificial Intelligence tools and methods in academic teaching (didactics) and scientific research.

## Course Overview

This course equips educators and researchers with practical knowledge and skills to leverage AI tools effectively in their professional work. Participants will learn to use AI for lesson planning, content creation, student support, literature review, data analysis, and more — while critically evaluating the ethical implications of AI adoption in academia.

**Target audience:** University educators, researchers, PhD students, and academic professionals  
**Duration:** 5 modules (~20 hours of learning)  
**Format:** Self-paced / workshop-compatible  
**Language:** English

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

- Explain how modern AI systems (large language models, generative AI) work at a conceptual level.
- Select and apply appropriate AI tools for teaching tasks (lesson planning, content generation, formative assessment).
- Use AI tools to accelerate research workflows (literature search, summarisation, writing assistance, data analysis).
- Critically evaluate AI outputs and identify errors, hallucinations, and biases.
- Apply ethical guidelines and institutional policies for AI use in academia.
- Design assignments and assessments that foster AI literacy among students.
