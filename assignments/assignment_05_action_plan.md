# Assignment 05 — Personal Action Plan

**Related module:** Module 05  
**Format:** Structured action plan  
**Length:** Approximately 1–2 pages  
**Completion:** End of course

---

## Purpose

Create a concrete, realistic plan for integrating AI into your professional practice over the next three months. This final assignment is not assessed for ambition or comprehensiveness — it is assessed for specificity, realism, and genuine reflection on your own context.

---

## Instructions

Complete each section below. Be as specific as possible. Vague goals (e.g., "use AI more") are less useful than specific ones (e.g., "use Elicit to search for papers in my next literature review in September").

---

## Part 1: Starting Point

Before describing where you want to go, describe where you are now:

1. **Current AI use:** What AI tools do you currently use (if any) in your teaching or research? How regularly?
2. **Biggest barrier:** What is the main thing preventing you from using AI more effectively? (e.g., time, skills, uncertainty about policy, concerns about quality)
3. **Biggest opportunity:** Where could AI make the most meaningful difference to your work right now?
