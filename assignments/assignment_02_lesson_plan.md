# Assignment 02 — AI-Enhanced Lesson Plan

**Related modules:** Module 02, Module 05 (Workshop A)  
**Format:** Structured lesson plan document  
**Length:** Approximately 2–4 pages  
**Completion:** After Module 05

---

## Purpose

Design a complete lesson plan for one session in a course you teach or plan to teach, using AI tools as a significant part of the design process. This assignment demonstrates your ability to use AI effectively while applying your own professional judgment and disciplinary expertise.

---

## Requirements

Your submission must include all of the following components:

| Field | Your Response |

### 2. Lesson Plan

Provide a complete, time-structured plan for the session. Include:

- **Warm-up / hook** (5–10 min): How will you engage students at the start?
- **Main content** (varies): What will be taught? How? In what sequence?
- **Active learning activity** (10–20 min): What will students do to practise or apply?
- **Formative check or closing** (5–10 min): How will you gauge understanding?

### 3. Supporting Material

Include at least **one** of the following, generated with AI assistance and edited by you:

- A student handout (summary, diagram, or concept guide)
- A set of 5–10 discussion questions
- A short case study or scenario
- 5 multiple-choice knowledge-check questions with answer key
- A key terms glossary (8–10 terms)

### 4. Assessment Design

Describe or provide one assessment item for this session:

- What will students do to demonstrate they have met the learning objective?
- How does the assessment account for AI? (e.g., Are students permitted to use AI? Under what conditions? How will you verify learning?)
- Optional: Provide a simple rubric.

### 5. AI Use Reflection

Write 150–250 words addressing:

- Which parts of this lesson plan were generated with AI assistance?
- What prompts or tools did you use?
- What did you change or add from your own expertise?
- What did AI get wrong or miss?
- What did you verify, and how?

---

## Evaluation Criteria

This assignment is assessed for **completion and reflection quality**, not for perfection of the lesson plan.

| Criterion | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Completeness** | All required sections are present |
| **Educational soundness** | The plan reflects understanding of good teaching practice |
| **AI use transparency** | Clear description of how AI was and was not used |
| **Critical reflection** | Thoughtful analysis of AI's contribution and limitations |
| **Professional quality** | Materials are suitable for real use in the described context |

---

## Submission Format

Submit as a single document (PDF or Word/Google Docs) with all sections clearly labelled.
